AURA OS Emulator

This emulator is a simulation of AURA OS, a custom Android launcher designed to let users talk to their stuffed animal or TalkBear-powered voice device. AURA OS brings TalkBear technology directly into Android, giving your companion a voice, personality, and the ability to interact with you in real time.

Your PC must have:

  • Microphone (Required)
  • Webcam (Optional)
